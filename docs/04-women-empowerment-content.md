## PROGRAMS

### 1. Skill Development - "स्त्री शक्ती"

**Tailoring & Fashion Design**
- 6-month comprehensive course
- Industrial sewing machines provided
- Pattern making, stitching, designing
- Market linkages for selling products

**Beauty & Wellness Services**
- Parlor training (hair, makeup, skincare)
- Certification course
- Salon setup guidance
- Client acquisition support

**Handicrafts & Artisan Skills**
- Warli painting, pottery, embroidery
- Traditional crafts revival
- Online selling platforms training
- Exhibition participation

**Computer & Digital Skills**
- MS Office, Tally, basic programming
- Digital marketing for entrepreneurs
- E-commerce platform usage
- Social media for business

### 2. Entrepreneurship - "उद्यमिनी"

**Business Incubation**
- Business plan development
- Seed funding: ₹25,000 - ₹1,00,000
- Mentorship for 1 year
- Market linkages

**Self-Help Groups (SHGs)**
- Form mahila bachat gats
- Microfinance linkages
- Collective bargaining power
- 50+ SHGs formed

**"दीदी बाज़ार" - Women's Marketplace**
- Physical & online marketplace
- Products: Handicrafts, food items, textiles
- Buyer connections (retail, corporate gifting)
- Branding and packaging support

### 3. Health & Wellness - "आरोग्यम्"

**Reproductive Health Awareness**
- Menstrual hygiene management
- Sanitary pad distribution
- Family planning counseling

**Nutrition & Anemia Camps**
- Iron supplementation
- Balanced diet education
- Cooking demonstrations

**Cancer Screening**
- Breast and cervical cancer camps
- Early detection saves lives
- Free checkups

**Mental Health Support**
- Counseling services
- Stress management workshops
- Support groups

### 4. Safety & Legal Rights - "निर्भया"

**Self-Defense Training**
- Karate, Judo basics
- 3-month course
- Confidence building

**Legal Rights Workshops**
- Property rights, divorce laws
- Domestic violence protection
- Police-community interface

### 5. Leadership Development

**Panchayat Leadership Training**
- For women sarpanchs and members
- Public speaking workshops
- Governance and administration

**Political Participation**
- Voter awareness
- Contesting elections guidance
- Women's collectives formation
