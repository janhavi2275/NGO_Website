## Social Media Content Plan

### Content Pillars (What to post about)
1. **Impact Stories** (40%) - Beneficiary success stories, transformations
2. **Program Updates** (25%) - New batches, events, milestones
3. **Behind-the-Scenes** (15%) - Team, field visits, day-in-the-life
4. **Educational Content** (10%) - Tips, awareness, information
5. **Engagement** (10%) - Questions, polls, user-generated content

### Posting Frequency
- **Facebook**: 3-4 times/week
- **Instagram**: Daily (posts + stories)
- **Twitter**: 2-3 times/day
- **LinkedIn**: 2-3 times/week
- **YouTube**: 1-2 videos/month

### Content Types
- Photos with captions
- Infographics (impact numbers, program info)
- Short videos (testimonials, event highlights)
- Carousels (multi-image posts)
- Stories (daily updates, behind-the-scenes)
- Reels/Shorts (trending, engaging content)
