Our Rural Transformation Model

We believe in a holistic 'Adarsh Gram' (Model Village) approach that integrates economy, ecology, and technology.

Sustainable Agriculture

Moving from traditional to profitable and sustainable farming practices.

  • Zero-Budget Natural Farming labs
  • Market linkages for organic produce
  • Soil health card distribution

Water Security

Enabling villages to become water-tanker free through community participation.

  • Desilting of old village ponds
  • Continuous contour trenching (CCT)
  • Micro-irrigation adoption support

Digital Empowerment

Bringing the power of the internet and e-governance to the village center.

  • Village Digital Service Centers
  • Online banking & UPI training
  • E-learning for rural youth

Clean Energy & Infra

Modernizing village infrastructure with a focus on sustainability.

  • Solar street light installation
  • Paver block roads for hamlets
  • Biogas promotion for kitchens

A Tale of Transformation

The story of Shirala - Our first Model Village

From Drought to Abundance

Shirala village faced acute water shortage for 20 years. Farmers were migrating to cities. Navinya intervened in 2021.

Through our integrated approach, we worked with the local youth to desilt two large ponds and implement rainwater harvesting. Today, Shirala has year-round water supply, and farmers have started growing high-value vegetable crops.
