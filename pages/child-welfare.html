Our Child Welfare Programs

A holistic approach to child development focusing on health, protection, and opportunities.

Nutrition & Health

We provide essential nutritional support to Anganwadis, including supplements and regular growth monitoring to combat malnutrition in rural children.

Child Protection

Awareness campaigns on child rights, anti-child labor initiatives, and abuse prevention workshops for children, parents, and community leaders.

Holistic Development

Fostering talents through sports, arts, music, and personality development programs to ensure all-round growth beyond academics.

Special Needs Support

Disability assessment camps, special education resources, and provision of assistive devices for children with different abilities.
