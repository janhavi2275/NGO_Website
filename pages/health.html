Healthier Communities, Better Lives

Providing accessible healthcare and sanitation solutions to the heart of rural Maharashtra.

Our Comprehensive Healthcare Model

From preventive care to emergency support, we bring quality healthcare to the doorstep of rural families.

Mobile Health Vans (MHVs)

Our "Hospital on Wheels" bridges the last-mile gap. Each van is staffed with a doctor, nurse, and lab technician.

  • Free consultations and medicines
  • Basic diagnostic tests (Blood, Sugar, BP)
  • Maternal & Child Health monitoring

Clean Water & Sanitation

We work toward open-defecation-free villages and sustainable water management.

  • Construction of household toilets
  • Community RO water filter plants
  • Waste segregation training

Mental Health Support

Breaking the stigma around mental health in rural areas through counseling and community support.

  • Farmers' distress counseling
  • Youth career-stress guidance
  • 24/7 Helpline availability

Menstrual Hygiene (Pankh)

Empowering rural women and girls with knowledge and access to sanitary products.

  • Sanitary pad distribution drives
  • Menstrual awareness workshops
  • Low-cost pad manufacturing units
