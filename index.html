ABOUT US

नवीन्य - एक नई शुरुआत का वादा
A Promise of New Beginnings

Navinya Foundation was born from a simple belief: every child, every woman, every family in rural Maharashtra deserves access to opportunities that can transform their lives.

Founded in 2022 by a group of educators and social workers who witnessed firsthand the struggles of talented students held back by poverty, we set out to bridge the gap between potential and possibility.

What makes us different? We combine grassroots understanding with modern technology, traditional wisdom with innovative solutions, and most importantly - we measure every rupee's impact. Because we believe that transparency and results are the foundation of trust.
