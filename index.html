About Navinya Foundation

Creating self-sustaining ecosystems for education, skills, livelihood, and dignity in rural Maharashtra.

Founded in 2022, Navinya Foundation ("नवीन्य" meaning "New Beginning") works across 7 strategic pillars to transform rural communities. We believe every person deserves a chance to realize their full potential, regardless of where they were born.
